# Champions League Semi-Final Drama: Real Madrid Edge Past Bayern

On **May 28, 2025**, at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, Real Madrid produced a late comeback to defeat Bayern Munich 2–1 on the night and 3–2 on aggregate, booking their place in the Champions League final for the first time since 2022.

**Key moments:**
- **Opening goal (12’) – Thomas Müller:** The veteran attacker struck with a low drive beyond Thibaut Courtois to give Bayern an early lead.  
- **Equaliser (68’) – Vinícius Júnior:** A dazzling solo run ended with a precise finish to restore parity.  
- **Decisive strike (89’) – Jude Bellingham:** The English midfielder curled a stunning effort into the top corner, sending the home crowd into delirium.

**Tactical battle:**  
Carlo Ancelotti’s decision to field a back three and overload the midfield proved decisive against Julian Nagelsmann’s high-pressing side. Real’s dominance in possession (60%) and six shots on target contrasted with Bayern’s lethal counter-attacks.

> “This is what the Champions League is all about,” said Ancelotti. “Our players showed heart and belief until the final whistle.”

The final will be held on June 1, 2025, at the Stade de France in Paris, where Real Madrid will face either Paris Saint-Germain or Inter Milan.
